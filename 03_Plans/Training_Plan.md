# Security and Compliance Training Plan

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** December 2025  
**Owner:** EcoMetricx InfoSec Team (Andrei Ionete - andrei@ecometricx.com)  
**Review Cycle:** Annually

## 1. Purpose

This training plan establishes security and compliance training requirements for all EcoMetricx personnel to ensure awareness of security policies, procedures, and best practices. This plan supports SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria CC2.1 (Communication and Information).

## 2. Scope

This plan applies to:
- All employees
- All contractors
- All vendors with system access
- All third parties with data access

## 3. Training Objectives

- Ensure personnel understand security policies and procedures
- Promote security awareness and best practices
- Reduce security risks through education
- Meet compliance and regulatory requirements
- Foster a security-conscious culture

## 4. Training Requirements

### 4.1 Mandatory Training

**All Personnel:**
- Information Security Awareness (annual)
- Acceptable Use Policy (annual)
- Data Classification and Handling (annual)
- Incident Reporting (annual)
- Phishing Awareness (annual)

**Role-Specific Training:**
- Administrators: Advanced security training
- Developers: Secure coding practices
- Data handlers: Data protection training
- Managers: Security management training

### 4.2 Training Frequency

- **New Hire Training:** Within 30 days of hire
- **Annual Training:** All personnel annually
- **Role-Specific Training:** As required by role
- **Policy Updates:** Within 30 days of policy update
- **Incident-Based Training:** Following security incidents
