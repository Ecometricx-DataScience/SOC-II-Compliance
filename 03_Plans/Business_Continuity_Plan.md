# Business Continuity Plan

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** December 2025  
**Owner:** EcoMetricx Management  
**Review Cycle:** Annually or following major organizational changes

## 1. Purpose

This Business Continuity Plan (BCP) establishes procedures to ensure EcoMetricx can continue critical business operations during and after a disruption. This plan supports SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria for Availability and complements the Disaster Recovery Plan.

## 2. Scope

This plan applies to:
- All critical business processes
- All critical information systems
- All business functions
- All personnel
- All locations

## 3. Business Impact Analysis

### 3.1 Critical Business Processes

**Tier 1 - Critical (RTO: < 4 hours):**
- Customer-facing applications
- Payment processing
- Data processing services
- Customer support

**Tier 2 - Important (RTO: < 24 hours):**
- Internal business systems
- Reporting and analytics
- Development environments

**Tier 3 - Standard (RTO: < 72 hours):**
- Non-critical systems
- Administrative functions

### 3.2 Recovery Time Objectives (RTO)

- **Critical Systems:** 4 hours
- **Important Systems:** 24 hours
- **Standard Systems:** 72 hours

### 3.3 Recovery Point Objectives (RPO)

- **Critical Systems:** 1 hour
- **Important Systems:** 24 hours
- **Standard Systems:** 72 hours
