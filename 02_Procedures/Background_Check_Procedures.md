# Background Check Procedures

**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** EcoMetricx InfoSec Team (Andrei Ionete - andrei@ecometricx.com)  
**Based on:** Access Control Policy, Information Security Policy

---

## 1. Purpose

Establish procedures for conducting background checks on personnel who will have access to EcoMetricx systems, data, or facilities.

## 2. Scope

Applies to:
- All new employees
- Contractors with system access
- Third-party personnel with data access
- Personnel with access to Confidential or Restricted data

## 3. Background Check Requirements

### 3.1 Standard Background Check (All New Hires)

Required checks:
- [ ] Identity verification
- [ ] Employment history (past 5 years)
- [ ] Education verification
- [ ] Criminal background check (per applicable law)
- [ ] Reference checks (2 minimum)

### 3.2 Enhanced Background Check

Required for personnel with:
- Administrative access to systems
- Access to Restricted data (per Data Classification Policy)
- Financial system access
- Customer PII access

Additional checks:
- [ ] Credit history (where legally permitted)
- [ ] Extended criminal history (7-10 years)
- [ ] Additional reference checks

### 3.3 Contractor/Vendor Checks

Required:
- [ ] Vendor must confirm background checks on personnel
- [ ] Or EcoMetricx conducts checks directly
- [ ] Documented in vendor agreement

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Pre-Employment

1. Conditional offer extended pending background check
2. Candidate provides written consent
3. HR initiates background check with approved vendor
4. Results reviewed by HR within 5 business days
5. If adverse findings, review per Fair Credit Reporting Act
6. Final employment decision made

### 4.2 Review of Results

**Clear Results:**
- Proceed with employment
- Document completion

**Adverse Findings:**
- HR reviews with hiring manager
- Consider nature, relevance, and recency
- Follow adverse action procedures if required
- Document decision and rationale

### 4.3 Periodic Re-verification

- Positions with Restricted data access: Every 3 years
- All other positions: Upon role change to higher access

## 5. Confidentiality

- Background check results are Confidential
- Access limited to HR and hiring manager
- Stored securely per Data Classification Policy
- Retention per HR records policy

## 6. Documentation

Maintain records of:
- Consent forms
- Background check initiation
- Results summary (not full report after 1 year)
- Employment decision
- Adverse action notices (if any)
