# EcoMetricx Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 5, 2025  
**Owner:** EcoMetricx InfoSec Team (Andrei Ionete - andrei@ecometricx.com)

## 1. Introduction

EcoMetricx LLC ("EcoMetricx," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We may collect:
- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number, company name
- **Account Information:** Username, password (encrypted), account preferences
- **Business Information:** Company data, utility account information, energy consumption data
- **Technical Information:** IP address, browser type, device information, usage data

### 2.2 Data Classification

Per our Data Classification Policy, we classify data as:
- **Public:** Marketing materials, public documentation
- **Internal:** Internal communications, project information
- **Confidential:** Customer PII, financial data, proprietary information
- **Restricted:** Payment card data, health information, credentials

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our services
- Process and analyze energy data
- Communicate with you about our services
- Improve our products and services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect against fraud and security threats

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with service providers who assist in our operations:
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- **Communication:** Microsoft 365, Slack
- **Development:** GitHub, Atlassian Confluence

All third parties are bound by data protection agreements per our Vendor Management Policy.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by:
- Legal process or court order
- Government requests
- Protection of our rights and safety

## 5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- **Encryption:** AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring:** CloudWatch, CloudTrail logging
- **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II, aligned with ISO 27001

## 6. Data Retention

We retain personal data:
- **Active accounts:** Duration of the business relationship
- **Inactive accounts:** Per contractual requirements
- **Legal holds:** As required by law

Data is securely deleted when retention periods expire.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Object:** Object to certain processing activities
- **Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw previously given consent

### 7.1 How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at:
- **Email:** privacy@ecometricx.com
- **Security Contact:** security@ecometricx.com

We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:
