# Information Security Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** December 2025  
**Owner:** EcoMetricx InfoSec Team (Andrei Ionete - andrei@ecometricx.com)  
**Review Cycle:** Annually or following major organizational or regulatory change

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the foundation for EcoMetricx's information security program, defining the organization's commitment to protecting information assets and ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information systems. This policy supports all SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, vendors, and third parties
- All information systems, networks, applications, and data
- All physical and cloud infrastructure
- All business operations

## 3. Information Security Objectives

EcoMetricx is committed to:
- Protecting information assets from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction
- Ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information
- Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations
- Maintaining customer and stakeholder trust
- Continuously improving security posture

## 4. Information Security Principles

### 4.1 Confidentiality

- Information accessible only to authorized individuals
- Encryption for sensitive data in transit and at rest
- Access controls based on need-to-know
- Data classification and handling per Data Classification Policy

### 4.2 Integrity

- Information accurate and complete
- Protection against unauthorized modification
- Change management controls
- Audit trails for all changes

### 4.3 Availability

- Systems available to authorized users when needed
- Business continuity and disaster recovery plans
- Redundancy and backup systems
- Monitoring and incident response
